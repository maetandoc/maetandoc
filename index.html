Say Hello

Welcome to My Pocket Partner,
your virtual assistant and social media manager with a human touch!

We believe that mastering the art of cultivating genuine relationships online, and strengthening the bridge between you and your customers is the key to long-term success.

My Pocket Partner is an exclusive VA and social media company built up of creative entrepreneurs, storytellers, and artists who have a knack for small business, and have had experience in starting up their own ventures. Instead of “virtual assistants”, we usually tell people to think of us as “business partners” or “second-pairs-of-eyes” because we understand the tedious tasks involved with being an entrepreneur, and the struggles of keeping up with the ever-changing trends online.

If you’re excited, we’re excited for you! If something goes awry, we’ll help you to stay back on track!

My Pocket Partner specializes in branding, social media, copywriting, graphic design, and other executive assistance. If you’re a busy business owner who needs a partner to make sure that nothing falls through the cracks, let’s talk!
